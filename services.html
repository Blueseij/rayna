Our services at a glimpse

Due Diligence

While due diligence has become an obvious exercise ahead of investments, acquisitions and partnerships, there have been striking failures in this seemingly straightforward type of research – including FTX and Frank that missed arguably obvious red flags. We recognize that every story has two sides, so we focus on determining acceptable levels of risk that are proportionate to the client’s risk appetite and financial exposure.

Investigations

Our investigations are reliable, ethical and transparent. We aim to deliver purpose-driven, strategic and pragmatic intelligence. We understand the importance of probing the context and interpreting the meaning behind our finding that will guide the client’s course of action, as well as consider various outcomes, scenarios and suggesting next steps.

Asset Tracing

When it comes to asset tracing, our primary objectives is speed to prevent asset dissipation and concealment, as well as thoroughness of research by exploring not only publicly available information but also open but hard-to-access data, and being creative in identifying details through indirect sources, including other jurisdictions and touchpoints such as counterparties and regulatory entries.

Geo-political Analysis

Our geo-political analysis is focused on providing relevant, insightful and in-depth analysis that goes deeper than open sources and obvious conclusions to enable well-considered decisions.
